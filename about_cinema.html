Director: Katsuhiro Otomo

Genre(s): Science Fiction, Cyberpunk, Anime
Salty Says: This movie is a masterclass in every possible facet of filmmaking. The story is brilliant, the atmosphere is absolutely haunting, the soundtrack and effects could not be more fitting, and the animation is about as close as humanity will get to perfection. Go watch this; feel something. Additionally, if you are a native English speaker, I would highly recommend watching the English dub of this movie. It far outweighs the original Japanese audio for me personally, mainly because my brain just doesn't pick up on the nuances of how Japanese speakers convey various emotions.
