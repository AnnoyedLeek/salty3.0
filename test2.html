The installation you are about to scroll through is a hand-stitched outlet for many of my own perspectives, anxieties, and compulsions. It is a channel to express my hatred for capitalism, violence, and imperialism. This work plays out my greatest fears for when capitalism reaches its apex. I wish we did not all wake up to a world perpetually engulfed in blood, money, and fire.

While I hope that this is not our time's end for my own sake, divising and assembling this piece has allowed me to cope with the possible outcomes that may or may not lie ahead and what that means for all of us. I hope that it resonates with you and gives you comfort that, although our planet has plunged into turmoil, there is always a chance for regrowth and new life in the end.

There are many small details throughout the piece that you may not catch on the first scroll. I encourage you to revisit the work to find new details and messages. You may also hover your mouse over some of the animations to reveal hidden text.

For intended viewing purposes, this page should be viewed on a desktop computer. Please set your browser's zoom to an appropriate size that ensures you do not have to scroll horizontally. All images in this work have been optimized to reduce bandwidth usage.

PHASE 02 - YOU MUST RESIST CHANGE

How will the masses react to the jackboot bearing all its might onto their throats?

Will they react at all, or will they simply gasp for air?

How will they feel when their families have nothing?

...when they realize that they are slaves to industry yet their children still starve?

Will they still say nothing when they possess nothing?


What becomes of those who truly have nothing to lose?

Will they still believe that they have only the power to watch as their oceans swell and their land swelters?

Will they go quietly, dying with a huddled whimper?

Will they try to alter an inevitable trajectory?


Look inward...

What would you do if you had nothing to lose?
